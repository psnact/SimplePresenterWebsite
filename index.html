FAQ

Quick answers for group leaders and hosts.

What is SimplePresenter for?
It’s a lightweight app for Windows and macOS to help cell groups and Bible study nights present content clearly: scriptures, song lyrics, discussion prompts, and announcements.
Do I need internet during the meeting?
Not necessarily. Many groups prefer everything prepared ahead of time. If your workflow uses online content, you’ll want stable Wi‑Fi.
How do I show it on a TV or projector?
Connect your laptop via HDMI (or an adapter) and set your display to extend. Then run SimplePresenter and choose the correct screen for presenting.
Where can I get updates or report an issue?
For updates and downloads, check the GitHub Releases page. To report bugs or request features, open an issue on GitHub.
